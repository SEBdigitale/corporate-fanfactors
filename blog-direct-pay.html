Direct Pay

Legal listening is not a boring compliance detail. It is the foundation of trust.

FanFactors begins with a simple platform promise: anyone can listen to music legally, and every play or purchase is designed to pay the copyright owner directly. That promise makes the marketplace different because rights are not buried behind the experience — they are the experience.

When ownership is visible, artists and rights holders can price confidently. Fans can support responsibly. Fan Owners can sell approved music without confusing the market. Partners can understand how value moves.

Direct pay changes the conversation

Instead of asking only how many people heard the song, FanFactors asks stronger questions: who owns the value, who supported it, who sold it, who invited new fans and how quickly did the owner see the earning signal?
