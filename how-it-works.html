What gets measured

The platform turns behavior into product intelligence.

FanFactors can track the metrics that tell an artist whether fans are simply listening or truly converting into supporters.

Listen-to-Support Rate How often plays become purchases, tips, unlocks or direct support. support events ÷ legal plays
Repeat Support Rate How often the same fan comes back to support again. repeat supporters ÷ active supporters
FanScore Growth How quickly fans climb through verified actions. score gained per fan per period
Drop Conversion How well exclusive releases turn attention into value. drop buyers ÷ drop viewers
