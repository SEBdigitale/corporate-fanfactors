FAQ

FanFactors explained without the buzzwords.

These answers are written for website visitors, artists, fans, partners and press.

FanFactors is a music social media gamified marketplace where fans listen legally, support artists directly and build status through verified music engagement.

Traditional streaming focuses on access and play counts. FanFactors focuses on legal listening, copyright-owner payment logic, artist-set pricing, direct marketplace support and social fan identity.

The platform promise is that every legal play or purchase is designed to pay the copyright owner directly, with clear ownership mapping behind each music asset.

FanScore is a gamified identity system that recognizes meaningful fan actions such as legal listening, purchases, invites, challenges and repeat support.

Artists can track legal plays, support actions, purchases, pricing response, invite growth, fan cohorts, FanScore activity and repeat support signals.
