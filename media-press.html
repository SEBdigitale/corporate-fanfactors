Press summary

FanFactors in one paragraph.

FanFactors.com is a music social media gamified marketplace built to turn legal listening into direct creator value. Fans can listen to music legally, support artists through purchases and marketplace actions, earn status through FanScore and invites, and help artists grow. Artists set their own prices, keep control of their rights and gain real-time visibility into earning and fan-support signals.
