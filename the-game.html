Gamified music support

Make support visible, rewarding and fun.

Fans already compete over who discovered an artist first. FanFactors gives that energy a structured system of scores, levels, challenges and identity without making music feel transactional.

01

FanScore

A transparent score that rises through verified listening, purchases, invites and community actions.

02

Badges

Collectible proof of early support, release participation, streaks, challenges and artist-specific missions.

03

Artist Quests

Campaigns that ask fans to complete support actions around a release, event or drop.

04

Leaderboards

Ranked communities that show top supporters by artist, genre, city, campaign or invite tree.

05

Unlocks

Access, previews, shoutouts, drops or private moments that artists can attach to fan milestones.

06

Social Proof

Every badge and rank becomes a shareable signal that brings more fans into the artist economy.

Game mechanics with a purpose

Every mechanic points back to artist value.

The game layer is not decoration. It is a conversion engine that helps fans know what to do next and helps artists understand which actions create real support.

Game KPIs

Gamification should increase retention and support conversion.

The platform can measure whether game loops are helping fans become repeat supporters instead of passive listeners.

Quest Completion Rate How many fans finish artist missions. completed quests ÷ started quests
Badge Share Rate How often earned status spreads socially. shared badges ÷ earned badges
Rank Retention Whether ranked fans return and support again. returning ranked fans ÷ ranked fans
Invite Lift How much game status increases invite activity. invites from ranked fans vs baseline
