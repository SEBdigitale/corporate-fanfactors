The invitation

FanFactors is not a streaming clone. It is a creator-marketplace rebellion.

The platform is built for people who believe music has more value than passive plays, more emotion than anonymous metrics and more commercial potential than one-size-fits-all pricing.

01

Artists: control the price

Stop waiting for platforms to define your value. Build your catalog, your drops and your fan market.

02

Fans: make support count

Listen legally, buy directly, earn status and help the artists you love grow.

03

Copyright owners: get recognized

Value should point to the owner behind the music with clear, measurable payment logic.

04

Labels: activate catalogs

Use social proof, quests and direct fan conversion to create new demand around music assets.

05

Creators: launch moments

Turn songs into campaigns, bundles, access passes and fan missions.

06

Partners: back a better model

Support a platform where product growth and creator economics are aligned by design.
