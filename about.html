Our mission

Turn music from passive content into a creator-powered marketplace.

FanFactors exists to help artists and copyright owners earn more clearly from the fans who already love their work. The product combines legal listening, direct payment logic, artist-set pricing, gamified social engagement and marketplace support.

01

Rights-respecting by design

Music access should not require shortcuts. Legal listening is the foundation.

02

Creator value at the center

The copyright owner should be visible in the economic design of the platform.

03

Fan support made visible

Fans should be able to see, share and celebrate the impact of their support.

04

Marketplace thinking

Music can be sold, bundled, unlocked and experienced in ways that artists control.

05

Real-time momentum

Artists should see earning and support signals while campaigns are alive.

Movement energy
